Services

Come on a learn to surf journey of a lifetime in Manu Bay as we set off on a half day adventure. Sit back and relax after we pick you up from your accommodation and begin the search. All package deals pick you up from your location and drop you off at the surf school. Get ready for your surf adventure by clicking here.

We provide all equipment for each lesson including boards and wetsuits to keep you warm on cold days.

With each lesson we cover Ocean safety to ensure you have the knowlede of where is safe to surf.

Your will receive half price dinner voucher and complimentary drink at local restaurant for each lesson.

Each session is four hours long, done in groups with our instructors, and are taught in waist deep water.

Each course will work on outback surfing techniques and experiment with different surfboards.

About

As a mobile surf school we are dedicated to finding the best, fun and safest waves daily. Our lessons are specifically designed to teach you to surf at your own pace in a relaxed environment to build further self-confidence.

We offer programs surfers of any age.

We teach beginners, intermediate and advanced.

We have over 10 different locations.

We have the best beaches in New Zealand.

We have over 80 fun safe soft surfboards.

We offer intermediate to advanced boards.

Instructors

We are the only Manu Bay surf school to have surf school permits to teach personal private lessons & group lessons in both Manu & Whale Bay.

Our Coaches have 10+ years of coaching background and 15 years personal surfing experience both in New Zealand and overseas.

Our coaches have a vast knowledge of the local area and will only conduct lessons in surf conditions in waist deep water

Our coaches have coaching accreditation and APOLA Beach lifeguard certification
